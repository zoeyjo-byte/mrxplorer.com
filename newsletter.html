AI Tips for MRX · Issue #4

Turn a discussion guide into a screener in about 60 seconds

You've already done the hard work of writing a discussion guide. The screener is mostly the same information — just reoriented toward filtering, not exploring. Here's the prompt I use:

You are a qualitative research expert. I'll paste a discussion guide below. Your job is to extract the core respondent criteria from it and write a screener questionnaire that would find the right people for this research. Include a flow logic recommendation (continue/terminate) for each question. Keep the screener to 10 questions or fewer. [paste your discussion guide here]

Adjust the question limit based on your panel's requirements. Works in Claude or ChatGPT.
