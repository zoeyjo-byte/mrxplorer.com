Common Questions

Everything leaders
need to know

What time zone are classes in?
All classes are live virtual and taught in Pacific Time. Individual sessions are at 12pm Pacific. The Leadership cohort meets at 9am Pacific.
How big are the Leaders cohort sessions?
Individual class sessions are capped at 12 registrants. The Leadership cohort is capped at 9 registrants to keep the experience collaborative and hands-on.
Can my company pay by invoice?
Yes. Contact Z directly at zjohnson@mrxplorer.com to arrange invoicing for organization-wide training.
Is there team pricing?
For teams larger than the cohort cap, the offering is a private team session scheduled separately. Contact Z to scope pricing and availability — private sessions are priced per engagement, not per seat.
Will I have to share my team's real workflows?
No. The workflow-mapping session uses a provided synthetic/redacted workflow designed for the group exercise. Your team's real workflows and proprietary data stay private.
Are classes recorded?
Yes. Classes are recorded and the recording is provided only to those who registered for that session. If you cannot attend live, you still receive the recording.
What is the refund policy?
Full refund available up to 72 hours before the scheduled start time. No refunds inside the 72-hour window.
