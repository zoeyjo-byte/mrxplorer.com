Common Questions

Quick answers to
common questions

How do I know which tier is right for me?
Beginner if you're new to AI or only use the free version of ChatGPT/Claude. Intermediate if you use AI day-to-day and want to level up with agents, quant analysis, and automation. Leaders if you're a director or decision-maker who needs governance, risk frameworks, and a 90-day implementation plan for your team.
What's the difference between individual classes and a cohort?
Individual classes are single 90-minute sessions you register for one at a time (up to 12 students). Cohort bundles cover the same content as a multi-week program at a discounted rate (up to 9 students), taught live at 9am Pacific. Cohorts need at least 5 enrolled to run.
What time zone are classes in?
All classes are live virtual and taught in Pacific Time. Individual sessions are at 12pm Pacific. Cohort sessions meet at 9am Pacific.
Are classes recorded?
Yes. Classes are recorded and the recording is provided only to those who registered for that session. If you cannot attend live, you still receive the recording after the session.
How do I register?
Browse the full class catalog or the Leaders track, choose your classes, and click Register. You'll be taken to our secure checkout to complete your purchase. Prices shown are tax-exclusive; applicable sales tax is calculated at checkout.
